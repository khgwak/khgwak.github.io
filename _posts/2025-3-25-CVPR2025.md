Our paper, *BIGS: Bimanual Category-agnostic Interaction Reconstruction from Monocular Videos via 3D Gaussian Splatting*, has been accepted to <a href="https://cvpr.thecvf.com/virtual/2025/poster/34060" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CVPR 2025</a>! [<a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2504.09097" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Paper</a>][<a href="https://github.com/On-JungWoan/BIGS" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Code</a>]

This paper presents a method for reconstructing bimanual object interactions from monocular videos. We propose a category-agnostic approach using 3D Gaussian Splatting to model both hands and objects without relying on prior object templates.
